If for some reason, you may want to stop your experiment before it finishes.

:::info
Your experiment needs to be currently running for you to be able to stop it. You cannot stop _Completed_ or _Failed_ experiments.
:::

## Quick guide

<VideoPlayer src="https://cdn.csquare.run/docs/webm/stop-experiment.webm" />

## Step-by-step

1. [Go to the experiment page](go-to-the-experiment-page).
2. Click on the `Stop` button on the `Experiment` widget.
