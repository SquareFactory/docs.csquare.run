The first step in order to use the csquare platform is to create an organization.
Be default, a personal organization is automatically created for you.

## Quick guide

<VideoPlayer src="/videos/organizations-create-an-organization.webm" />

## Step-by-step

1. Go to the `Settings` page using the header.
2. Click on the `Organizations` tab on the left.
3. Click on the `+` button: `Create organization`.
4. Fill the form: type the name of your new organization in the `Name field`.
5. Click on the `Create` button.

That's it: you just created your new organization!
