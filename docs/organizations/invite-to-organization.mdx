## Step-by-step
1. Access the `Organization` page using the header.
2. Select the project you want to work with using the dropdown.
3. Click on the `Invite to organization button`.
4. Fill the form:
    1. Select the permissions of your future colleagues using the `Permissions` button.
    2. Indicate their email addresses in the `Email address` fields. You can add fields by clicking on the `+` button at the end of the row.
5. Click on the submit button.
6. Invitations will be sent at the email addresses you indicated earlier.
