## Step-by-step

:::warning

Only administrators of an organization can remove collaborators from said organization.

:::

1. #### Select the organization in which you want to remove a collaborator.
2. #### Go to the `Collaborators` page.
3. #### Click on the `Delete` button of the corresponding collaborator's line.
4. #### Click on the `Delete permanently` button.
