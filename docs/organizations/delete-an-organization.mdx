:::warning

By removing an organization, it will become unavailable to all users and cannot be recovered. All the projects, project's models and orphans datasets are deleted.

:::

## Quick guide

<VideoPlayer src="https://storage.googleapis.com/cdn.csquare.run/docs/webm/delete-organization.webm" />

## Step-by-step

1. #### Go to the `Settings` page using the header.
2. #### Click on the `Organizations` tab on the left.
3. #### Select the organization you want to remove in the list.
4. #### Click on the `Delete` button.
5. #### Fill the form: enter the organization's ID on the confirmation field.
6. #### Click on `Delete permanently`.

That's it, great job!
