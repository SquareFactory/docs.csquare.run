:::warning

Once deleted, a model becomes unavailable to all projects and users and cannot be recovered.

:::

## Quick guide

<VideoPlayer src="https://cdn.csquare.run/docs/webm/delete-a-model.webm" />

## Step-by-step

1. [Select the project](../projects/select-a-project.mdx) that contains the model you want to delete.
2. Go to the `Models` page using the sidebar.
3. Click on the `Delete` button on the corresponding row.
4. Fill the form by typing your model's id.
5. Click on `Delete permanently`.

You can now see that your model is not displayed in the **models table** anymore. Great work!
