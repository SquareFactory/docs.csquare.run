:::info

Your experiment needs to be currently running for you to stop it. You cannot stop *Completed* or *Failed* experiments.

:::

## Quick guide
<VideoPlayer src="https://storage.googleapis.com/cdn.csquare.run/docs/webm/stop-an-experiment.webm"/>

## Step-by-step
1. [Go to the experiment page](go-to-the-experiment-page).
2. Click on the `Stop` button on the `Experiment` widget.
