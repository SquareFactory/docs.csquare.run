## Step-by-step
1. [Select the project](../projects/select-a-project) you want to work with.
2. Go to the `Labs` page using the sidebar.
3. Click on the `Delete` button on the corresponding line.
4. Fill the form by writing the lab's ID in the field.
5. Click on the `Delete permanently` button.

Your lab has been deleted. Well done!
