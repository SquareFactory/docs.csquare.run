## Step-by-step
1. #### [Select the project](../projects/select-a-project) you want to work with.
2. #### Go to the `Labs` page using the sidebar.
3. #### Start the lab:
    - ##### From the `Labs` page
        1. Click on the `Start` button on the corresponding line.
    - ##### From a specific lab's page
        1. Click on the ID of the lab you want to work with to access its dedicated page.
        2. Click on the `Start` button.
4. #### You're done: good job!
