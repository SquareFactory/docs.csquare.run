:::warning
Once deleted, a project becomes unavailable to all users and cannot be recovered. All the project's
models and orphans datasets are also deleted.
:::

## Quick guide

<VideoPlayer src="/videos/projects-delete-project.webm" />

## Step-by-step

1. Go to the `Settings` page.
2. Click on the `Projects` tab on the left.
3. Select the project you want to delete in the list.
4. Click on the `Delete` button on the corresponding line.
5. Fill the confirmation form by entering the project's ID.
6. Click on the `Delete permanently` button.

You can now see that your project is not displayed in the projects table anymore. Nicely done!
